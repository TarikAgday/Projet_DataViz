MAJOR LEAGUE SOCCER 2019 SEASON ANALYSIS

BUDGETS BY TEAMS 24 teams evenly spread in the West and Eastern conference were fighting for the 14 playoffs spots.

This first visualization demonstrates the budget of teams and their standings. The average budget per team was 12.3M$.

While the general standing affects playoffs access, the main objective of teams is not to be the best team in the ranking but ultimately to win the playoffs. The button in the legend switches between the general rankings and the playoffs' standings. The graph is separated by 4 rectangles. The green and light yellow rectangle encompasses the teams that made the playoffs and had respectively a budget below and over the average while the red and darker yellow reactangle represents teams that did not qualify for the playoffs.
SALARIES BY AGE GROUPS

The second graph is a heatmap of the salaries by group ages. In its early years, most of the best players of the MLS used to be at the end of the career (around 33-35 years old). However, the average age of the league was 25,95 years old in 2019, which is relatively young compared to the English Premier League who attracts most of the best players (27,3 years old). Also, this graph shows us a tendance to invest more money on young players.
